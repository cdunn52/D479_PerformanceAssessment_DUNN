Taniti Island Dining

Restaurants

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

  • Local Fish & Rice: The Driftwood Grill, Coral Catch, Island Ember, Taniti Tide Table, Bluefin Bayhouse
  • American-Style: The Palm Diner, Taniti Smokehouse, Sunset Burgers & Brews
  • Pan-Asian: Lotus Lantern, Wok & Wave

Grocery Stores

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.

  • Supermarkets: Taniti Market Central, Island Fresh Supermart
  • Smaller Grocery Stores: Lagoon Provisions, Harbor Basket
  • 24-Hour Convenience: QuickShell Express
